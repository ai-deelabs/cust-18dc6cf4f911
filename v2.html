Sabai, sabai

Wellness in the old, gentle way.

At Super Spa, we follow the Thai philosophy of sabai — a state of ease in body and mind. Every treatment is paced to the rhythm of your breath, never the clock.

We blend hand-picked herbs, warm oils, and time-honoured pressure work to coax tension from where it hides. You arrive carrying the day; you leave lighter than you came.

  • Botanical & natural. Herbal compresses and oils blended in-house, free from synthetics.
  • Trained Thai therapists. Lineage techniques passed through generations of practice.
  • A truly quiet space. Low light, soft sound, and time that belongs only to you.
